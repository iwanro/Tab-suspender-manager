# Privacy Policy - Tab Suspender & Manager

**Last Updated:** December 23, 2025

## Overview

Tab Suspender & Manager ("the Extension") is a browser extension designed to help users manage their browser tabs more efficiently. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Local Browser Data

The Extension stores data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data includes:

- **Tab Information**: URLs and titles of tabs you save in sessions
- **User Settings**: Your preferences for auto-suspension, dark mode, and other settings
- **Saved Sessions**: Groups of tabs you choose to save for later restoration

**Important**: All data is stored **locally on your device** and is never transmitted to external servers.

### 2. Browser Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions to function:

- **tabs**: To access and manage your browser tabs
- **tabGroups**: To organize tabs into groups
- **storage**: To save your settings and sessions locally
- **alarms**: To schedule automatic tab suspension
- **notifications**: To notify you when tabs are suspended

## How We Use Your Information

### 1. Tab Management
- Access tab URLs and titles to save and restore sessions
- Suspend inactive tabs to reduce memory usage
- Group tabs by domain for better organization

### 2. User Preferences
- Store your settings (auto-suspension intervals, whitelist domains, etc.)
- Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode)

### 3. Local Storage Only
- All data remains on your device
- No data is sent to any external server
- No analytics or tracking

## Data Retention

The Extension retains your saved sessions and settings until you:
- Delete them through the Extension's interface
- Uninstall the Extension
- Clear your browser data

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:
- All data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage API
- The Extension does not collect or transmit personal data
- No third-party services have access to your data

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising platforms.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store extension support page.

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored by the Extension through Chrome's storage inspection
- **Delete**: Remove saved sessions and settings through the Extension interface
- **Export**: Your data is stored locally and can be accessed through Chrome's developer tools

## Compliance

This Extension complies with:
- Google Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
- GDPR (for users in the European Union)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
